Little about me

Hi there! I am a computer science student at the Krakow University of Technology. My passion for technology began during my school years when I decided that my future lies in the field of IT. Currently, I am intensively developing my skills to succeed in this dynamically evolving domain.

I am passionate about repairing and diagnosing various electronic devices. I enjoy challenges associated with reviving items that may seem lost. Moreover, my interests include creating DIY devices and constructing with Lego bricks. This is not only a form of entertainment but also a way to develop creativity and manual skills, and at the same time, a way to create something useful from scratch.

Since obtaining my driver's license and buying my first car, I have discovered a fascination with automotive mechanics, although I am not a professional mechanic. Minor repairs and modifications to my car provide great satisfaction.

To maintain physical activity and avoid being sedentary, I go on mountain trips, which are a leisurely form of recreation for me. Additionally, the gym is a place where I focus on physical fitness, complementing outdoor activities. The gym also has an additional advantage for me. It allows me to decompress, mentally unwind through exercise. It helps clear my mind of negative and intrusive thoughts and has often allowed me to become a better version of myself.

I am adaptable, which allows me to quickly adapt to changing work conditions and diverse tasks that I encounter. My ability to quickly grasp new technologies enables me to effectively utilize the latest tools and solutions in my field. I always strive to approach problems and challenges in a creative manner, seeking unconventional solutions that can bring positive results both in my professional and personal life. I believe that flexibility and openness to new ideas are key to achieving success in today's rapidly changing world. I am committed to continuous improvement to be prepared for the new challenges and opportunities that the future holds.
